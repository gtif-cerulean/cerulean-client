CIF Background ​

The Cerulean Information Factory (CIF) is a demonstration project funded by the European Space Agency (ESA) to develop tools and capabilities that can assist marine industries in supporting a transition to a more sustainable future. CIF is named after the blue-green colour, reflecting the need to combine the Blue and Green Economies in order to have a successful Green Transition. The CIF project is part of the ESA “Green Transition Information Factories” (GTIF) initiative https://gtif.esa.int/. The intent is to use Earth Observation (EO) data, geospatial and digital platform technologies, and advanced analytics to generate actionable knowledge and decision support for climate change impact reduction and adaptation.

CIF Focus ​

The CIF demonstration focus region is the North Atlantic and Arctic seas between Canada and Europe.

Focus Region

The CIF demonstration provides examples relevant to three topics: Arctic shipping, offshore wind energy, and aquaculture.

  • Application development
  • Execution environment
  • Data storage and management
